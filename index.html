STAY - because everyone deserves an education.

Even those who can't afford one.

How does it work?

Stay makes it easy for teachers to track absence and help us prevent dropouts.
Paired with our local systems to swiftly follow up long spanning absence, Stay helps communities keep their children in school.

The App

With our user friendly interface, the app allows the teacher to report who's missing and the opportunity to specificy reason of absence.
The app will have accesible statistics of every students attendance, so it's easy to keep track of who needs extra attention.
